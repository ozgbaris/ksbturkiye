Frequently asked questions

Who is KSB?

KSB has been a global leader in pump and valve technology since 1871.

KSB offers high-quality, long-lasting and energy-efficient solutions for a wide range of applications, from industrial plants to city water networks, from buildings to power plants. With an extensive worldwide service network and modern production facilities, KSB provides reliable service with customer satisfaction at the forefront.

KSB's global engineering team and local service centers provide customers with the highest level of support in maintenance, repair, commissioning and technical consultancy.

When was KSB founded?

KSB has been a global leader in pump and valve technology since 1871.

KSB is headquartered in Frankenthal, Germany and is present in more than 100 countries worldwide. With around 16,000 employees, KSB offers its customers high-quality pump, valve and service solutions. The product range includes industrial pumps, circulators, multistage pumps, booster systems and various valve types.

KSB's global production network offers fast and efficient solutions all over the world thanks to strategic production sites in Europe, Asia, America and Africa. To better serve its customers, KSB provides industry-specific engineering services and a comprehensive after-sales service network.

Does KSB have a certificate of superior quality standards?

Has KSB achieved great success?

The KSB Group is one of the leading suppliers of quality pumps, valves and related services with annual sales revenues of around EUR 3 billion and a presence on all continents with its own sales and marketing organizations, production facilities and service operations. KSB employs more than 16,000 people.

The company's success is based on innovative technology, the fruit of its own global research and development activities. In addition to customers and employees, KSB also serves society at large. Examples of our social responsibility activities include environmental and educational projects. We are committed to the United Nations Global Compact. Its principles promote social, environmental and economic justice worldwide.
